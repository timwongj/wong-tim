Notation

Face Turns

U - Turn the upper face clockwise 90 degrees

D - Turn the bottom face clockwise 90 degrees

F - Turn the front face clockwise 90 degrees

B - Turn the back face clockwise 90 degrees

L - Turn the left face clockwise 90 degrees

R - Turn the right face clockwise 90 degrees

Double Layer Turns

u - Turn the upper face and the middle layer next to it clockwise 90 degrees

d - Turn the down face and the middle layer next to it clockwise 90 degrees

f - Turn the front face and the middle layer next to it clockwise 90 degrees

b - Turn the back face and the middle layer next to it clockwise 90 degrees

l - Turn the left face and the middle layer next to it clockwise 90 degrees

r - Turn the right face and the middle layer next to it clockwise 90 degrees

Slice Moves

M - Rotate the cube along the x-axis clockwise 90 degrees

E - Rotate the cube along the y-axis clockwise 90 degrees

S - Rotate the cube along the z-axis clockwise 90 degrees

Rotations

x - Rotate the cube along the x-axis clockwise 90 degrees

y - Rotate the cube along the y-axis clockwise 90 degrees

y - Rotate the cube along the z-axis clockwise 90 degrees

Counter-Clockwise and Double Turns

If a letter is follow by a ', turn that side counter-clockwise 90 degrees

If a letter is follow by a 2, turn that side 180 degrees
