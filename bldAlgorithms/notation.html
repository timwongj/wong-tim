3x3 Notation

U - Turn the up side clockwise 90 degrees

D - Turn the down side clockwise 90 degrees

F - Turn the front side clockwise 90 degrees

B - Turn the back side clockwise 90 degrees

L - Turn the left side clockwise 90 degrees

R - Turn the right side clockwise 90 degrees

x - Rotate the cube along the x-axis clockwise 90 degrees

y - Rotate the cube along the y-axis clockwise 90 degrees

y - Rotate the cube along the z-axis clockwise 90 degrees

If a letter is follow by a ', turn that side counter-clockwise 90 degrees

If a letter is follow by a 2, turn that side 180 degrees
